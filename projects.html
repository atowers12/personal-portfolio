Polly and Peter's Properties Management Portal

I308 Course Project · Spring 2024

Class project to design a full-stack web application backed by a relational database for the fictional client Polly and Peter's Properties. The system supports daily operations, reporting, and long-term planning.

  • Implemented modules for property maintenance, legal action tracking, lease management, and owner records.
  • Designed "Pricey Properties" insights to highlight revenue opportunities and surface at-risk assets.
  • Modeled database entities and relationships to keep business data consistent and auditable.
